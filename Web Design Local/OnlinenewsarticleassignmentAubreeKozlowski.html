Tragic Explosion at Local Pizza Shop

Two Lives, Several Injured

WEDNESDAY, APRIL 2nd 2025

A very devastating explosion occurred at the Local Pizza Shop in town late Tuesday evening, killing two people and injuring several others, including customers and staff. Authorities responded to a 911 call around 7:45 PM, after reports of a horrific explosion occurred. While others are to believe this was intentional on the Pizza Shops behalf, further investigation was prompted and the explosion was originated from the pizza oven, possibly due to a gas leak. Firefighters and emergency responders were quick to arrive on the scene, extinguishing the fire and securing the building. Sadly, two individuals, one of whom was a staff member and the other a customer, were pronounced dead at the scene. Several others were taken to the hospital with injuries ranging from burns to broken bones.

Later on at the scene witnesses described scenes of chaos as customers and employees scrambled to escape the building.
